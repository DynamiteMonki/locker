- Computer Science is all about taking **inputs** and ***processing*** them and give out a reasonable **output**.
- *Programming* is what you do to take the *input* in the desiered way and *process* the way you want and present the *output* you get from the *processing*.

---

# Why Programming ?

- ***Programming*** is a art of instructing computers to performa a task.
- Programming is done using a programming language, some are *python, go, rust and JavaScript*. 
	- Machines don't understand human language, and only process 0's and 1's
	- Programming languages bridge the gap between machines and humans by giving a human understandable **input system** and a machine understandable **output system**.
